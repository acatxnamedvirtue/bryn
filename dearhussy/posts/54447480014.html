The Radical Doula Guide

Persistence contributor Miriam Zoila Pérez has launched an exciting new project - The Radical Doula Guide.

Doulas provide emotional support during pregnancy and childbirth. The guide is a political primer to doula work as activism, and how being a doula connects to and must take into consideration things like race, class, sexuality, ability, immigration status and much, much more.
