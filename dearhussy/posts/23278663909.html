remember when we fucked on the beach last summer? the thing about fucking on the beach, or, for that matter, writing about fucking on the beach is that they’re both really cheesy things to do.

but suffice to say that it was very cool. i wondered what you thought when those random guys were watching us, circling around us not like sharks but like, i don’t know, pidgeons at the park when you sit down to eat a muffin, like how do they even know you’re eating a muffin, but there they are, wanting you to throw them a piece. these guys did not exactly want us to throw them a piece, but maybe they thought if they got close enough we might be like, hey, wanna join in. which is weird, too, in a way, because like we’re on the gay beach, so what do they even want with straight (“straight,” whatever) people fucking.
