i am currently experimenting with wearing a strong lip. specifically, i have been wearing MAC’s lady danger lip color, which is like an orange-ish red, which some people interpret as straight-up orange, which i attribute to natural variation in sensual perception. i mean, i know a couple people who are color-blind, so so what.

i totally ganked this lip color suggestion from mx. justin bond’s elegant slimoire, and i say, you know, what maybe works, works. i have shied away from intense reds in the past because most of them are blue-based, which is like, ugh, no. but i’m into this one, and it’s matte and hardcore.

WHICH I AM TELLING YOU BECAUSE it’s kind of a thing, a solidarity, to see another lady on the bus at 9am who is wearing astrong lip, it’s like, you go girl. it’s nine am and you’re not afraid to put it down on me (and everybody else.) (with your lips.)

in other news you did not need or really care to know, i have really fucked up toenails. like, everyone says their feet are weird, but mine are really weird. and, stubbornly, i continue to wear sandals and, nay, flip. flops. because, i mean, they are the best. and whenever i see a girl on public transportation with fucked up feet, i’m like, solidarity, girl.

so, this has been a vacuous post about femme clichés. i am one of those girls. but, whatever, it’s fun, and it has been fun to bore you with my personal grooming minutiae for like three minutes.
