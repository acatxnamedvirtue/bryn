HOW TO KEEP YOUR WINTER STOCKINGS FROM FALLING DOWN: A HUSSY TUTORIAL

So, the Hussy generally is against writing anything “useful” or “relevant,” but she had the following problem, and maybe you do too, and she has found a solution that she wants to share. The Hussy will admit she is a sucker for these American Apparel cable-knit stockings (I know, I know):

She wears them basically all winter long, including in the middle of Artic Vortex Janus. However, after you break them in a little (or even right after you buy them) you start having this problem:

They slide down! Maybe even farther! Maybe even down to your knees. Which in addition to looking all church lady, is *brrrrrr*. OF COURSE, The Hussy owns regular stocking garters, but they are meant for *delicate lacy things*, not serious yarn-based knitwear. So this crafty Hussy crafted a crafty plan!

I went to a deep discount store in my neighborhood and bought these cute boy shorts:

Note what I paid for them:

Then, I went to this discount menswear store in my neighborhood. I asked if they had suspenders, and of course they did, however, they were $15! That is too rich for the Hussy’s blood. However, I explained to them what I wanted to do, which was use the alligator clip and the elastic to make a garter. They immediately understood the project, and sold them to me for half price.

(And, in exchange for some light molestation, they even tailored them for free! However, the Hussy could have done that at home, to sew the elastic onto the short just takes an easy box stitch.)

So what we end up with is this cute situation! And I don’t have to worry about these bitches falling down anymore!:

I am even planning on painting the hardware some cute color with these two bottles of cheap nail polish I picked up on the way home ($1 each). Do you think I should?

BUDGET FOR THIS PROJECT:
- underz: $1
- suspenders + tailoring: $7
- two bottles of nail polish: $2
TOTAL COST: $10

Anyway, hope that was helpful. Maybe you will undertake a similar project! Or come up with an even better solution – if you do, lmk.

Happy slutting around and dressing inappropro for the weather!
